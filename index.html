No One’s Home is a psychological horror game where you’re left alone for the night—but something feels off. At first, it’s just small things: a misplaced object, a door slightly open when you swore it was closed. But soon, the house itself begins to feel… wrong. Unexplainable noises, shadows moving in the hallway, and cryptic messages on your phone make you question reality. Are you truly alone? Your only goal: Survive until morning.
